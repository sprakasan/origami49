Facts about camel:

  • There are two types of camels: humped camels or dromedary camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids.
  • Camels weight up to 1500 pounds.

Facts about panda:

  • Bamboo is a food pandas eat a lot.
  • They have great camouflage for their enviroment.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black/brown.

Facts about chameleon:

  • Chameleons have eyes on the backs of their heads.
  • Chameleons change skin color.
  • Chameleons like hot enviroments.
